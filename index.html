The Philippines is one of the most attractive countries with beautiful beaches, impressive mountains, and a rich cultural heritage. Every year, millions of tourists flock to the islands of the Philippines to explore its breathtaking beauty.

— Here are the top 4 best tourist attractions to visit in the Philippines! —

1. Mayon Volcano

Active volcano with perfect cone shape. Mayon Volcano is a famous stratovolcano in the Philippines, known for its symmetric conical shape. It is located in the province of Albay in the Bicol Region, on the large island of Luzon.

2. Intramuros

Historic walled city in Manila. Intramuros, also known as the Walled City, is a significant historical landmark in the Philippines. It is located in the capital city, Manila, and was the center of the country's political, economic, and religious life during the Spanish colonial period.

3. Taal Volcano

Small but active volcano in Batangas. Taal Volcano is a complex volcano located in the province of Batangas, on the island of Luzon in the Philippines. Despite its small size, it is one of the most active volcanoes in the country, with more than 30 historical eruptions.

4. Chocolate Hills

Unique geological formation in Bohol. The Chocolate Hills are a famous group of hills in the province of Bohol, Philippines. They are composed of more than 1,200 hills that cover an area of more than 50 square kilometers. The hills are covered in grass that turns brown during the dry season, giving them their name.
